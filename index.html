About me

Hello everyone!! Thank you for visiting my page. I am a graduate of BRAC University, Bangladesh, and currently working as a lecturer at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, BRAC University. I have done several projects in my undergrad journey, including building line-following robots, power generation modules with piezoelectric transducer, and creating websites like web messaging systems and cafeteria management systems using PHP, HTML, and CSS. I also developed an android app called Emergency Health Care app during the Covid-19 pandemic, which displays valuable information regarding corona updates. I also served as a Student Tutor (ST) for my department and was a merit-based scholarship holder during my undergrad studies.

I worked with Node.js, React.js, Express.js, MongoDB Atlas while building an full-stack web application. I am currently into Machine learning and Deep Learning architecture and working on a research paper. This domain of Science is fascinating to me, and I hope to conduct further researches in this domain.
