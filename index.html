About me

Hello everyone!! Thank you for visiting my webpage (I tried to keep it simple). I am currently pursuing a Master's in Computer Science at the University of Lethbridge (Focusing on Algorithmic Optimization). Earlier I completed a Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering at BRAC University, Bangladesh. I also served as an adjunct lecturer at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, BRAC University. before that, I worked as an IELTS instructor at Merseyside Consultancy, Chattogram for a few months. I have done several projects in my undergrad journey, including building line-following robots, power generation modules with piezoelectric transducer, and creating websites like web messaging systems and cafeteria management systems using PHP, HTML, and CSS. I also developed an Android app called Emergency Health Care app during the Covid-19 pandemic, which displays valuable information regarding coronavirus updates. I also served as a Student Tutor (ST) for computer science courses and was a merit-based scholarship holder during my undergrad studies.

I also worked with Node.js, React.js, Express.js, and MongoDB Atlas while building a full-stack web application. My interest dives into but is not limited to BlockChain, Machine learning and Deep Learning architecture and worked on several research papers related to the field. This domain of Science is fascinating to me, Moreover, I like to learn interesting new things and I hope to conduct further research on various domains and work on interesting projects.
